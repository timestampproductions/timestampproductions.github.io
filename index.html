An Síol is a Community Development Project (CDP) working in Stoneybatter for over 25 years.

We deliver services and provide support to meet the needs of the residents of Stoneybatter and the North West Inner City.
SERVICES:

We provide a wide range of free services such as:

  • Homework clubs, one to one tuition, early literacy supports and educational supports to children with special educational needs.

  • Meals and mini bus service, activities and a friendship service to senior citizens.

  • Advocacy for local residents and supports to tenants and residents groups.

  • A Welfare to Work Information Service.

  • Organisational and management supports to other community projects.

WELFARE RIGHTS INFORMATION:

An Siol provides a free, confidential and impartial welfare rights information service.

The aim of the service is to provide information, support, advocacy and assitance to local residents around their rights and entitlements to Social Services.

An Siol CDP Values:

Values are core to how and why we work in our community.

Our Core Values: Respect, Dignity and Empathy and for An Siol is an integral valued resource in the community.

These values are about every single person/group that has contact with An Siol CDP is treated in the same way, that they feel welcome and respected. We aim to assist them in any way we can or try to find an appropriate person or organisation that can assist their unique situation. We always strive to work to the highest professional and ethical standards.
